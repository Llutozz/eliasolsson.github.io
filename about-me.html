About Me


▶ Get to know me!

I am Swedish and 23 years old, just fresh of studying Game Design at PlaygroundSquad. I specialize in Level Design, but can dabble in all other parts of Game Design, particularly Gameplay/Systems. My gaming journey started in 2011 with Minecraft and Team Fortress 2 and I have continued ever since, my current favourite genre are simulators, particularly automation games.
I began making maps and missions for Team Fortress 2, which inspired me to puruse a game designer career and not too long after I also began modding Hearts of Iron IV. I went to Uppsala estetiska gymnasium between 2019 and 2022 and studied bits of game design. Since August 2023, I'm studying at PlaygroundSquad. Followng all this, I now consider myself a Level Designer, but also capable of Gameplay/Systems Design.
I always try to be motivated person with the intention to learn and strengthen my craft in any way I can.
If you have any questions or concerns, please read my About Me or my CV/Resume. But don't hesitate to contact me using my socials. I don't bite! Any feedback on this portfolio or any of my projects is also greatly appreciated! Thank you <3
